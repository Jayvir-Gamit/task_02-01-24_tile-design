ALLU ARJUN ESCAPES A DISASTER Jayvir Gamit Jan 05, 2023

Actor Sundeep Kishan starrer movie ‘Okka Ammayi Thappa’ which was released on 10th June Worldwide, received disaster talk on its opening day but earned Rs 1.3 Crores from both Telugu states Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It is directed by Rajasimha Thadinada in which actress Nithya Menon played the female lead role.

According to the sources, earlier the makers wanted to rope Mega Hero and eyed on Stylish Star Allu Arjun. Director Rajasimha Thadinada personally met Bunny and narrated the script. Although Bunny was impressed with story line but he felt that the script needs to be handled by any experienced director but Rajasimha was not ready to give the script to other director as he wanted to direct the movie himself. Finally the movie has been rejected by the audiences.
